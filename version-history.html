Version History

v1.1.0 - 2023-01-20

  • Added a main volume slider.
  • Added a ping counter.
  • Added support for numpad controls.
  • Improved the design of the bank vault menu.
  • Improved the FPS counter to not update so frequently.

v1.0.6 - 2023-01-11

  • Fixed retargeted attacks in battle to prioritize monsters of the same type.
  • Fixed party/trade/battle invites to only prompt players when they are not already in a battle.
  • Fixed party/trade/battle invites to immediately prompt players upon exiting a battle, if standing within range.
  • Fixed the battle enemy number labels to be aligned properly.

v1.0.5 - 2023-01-08

  • Fixed battle sometimes not playing out player ability selections properly.

v1.0.4 - 2023-01-08

  • Fixed a bug with shop UI which could cause the game to freeze.

v1.0.3 - 2023-01-08

  • Fixed AoE abilities not properly accounting for the amount of targets in their calculation of damage dealt.
  • Fixed the abilities list menu to not show MP cost when the cost of an ability is 0.

v1.0.2 - 2023-01-08

  • Fixed battle sometimes not playing out player ability selections properly.
  • Fixed random encounter to only roll for the party leader instead of every player in the party.
  • Fixed Escape not working properly.
  • Fixed players being able to send blank messages in chat.

v1.0.1 - 2023-01-07

  • Fixed the encounter formula to allow 3 free steps without encounters instead of 2.
  • Fixed certain drop tables in the overworld to drop their correct items instead of items from Goblin Cave.
  • Fixed selected item UI to show the correct level required to equip the item.
  • Fixed stats menu to show the time played per character instead of across all characters.
  • Fixed incorrect loop points and volume levels of certain music tracks.
  • Fixed typo "Waiting for other other players" in battle.
  • Fixed the player list to show players in alphabetical order.
  • Fixed incorrect hover graphics on subscription buttons.
