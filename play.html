Play

We strive to support most modern browsers with the web client, but the desktop client is the optimal way to play the game.

Play in the browser

Open in new tab

Controls

Desktop

  • Movement - WASD/Arrow keys
  • Turn - Move while holding Shift
  • Action - Spacebar
  • Select target in battle - Number keys
  • Open stats in world - Z
  • Open abilities in world - X
  • Open inventory in world - C
  • Attack in battle - E
  • Open abilities in battle - X
  • Open inventory in battle - C
  • Escape in battle - V
  • Global chat - Y
  • Local chat - U
  • Party chat - H
  • Trade chat - T
  • Refocus game from chat - Tab

Mobile

  • Movement - Joystick
  • Action - Press center of joystick

Instructions

  1. Download the correct version of the client for your operating system.
  2. Extract the contents of the zipped folder into a new directory.
  3. Run "RetroMMO" executable.
