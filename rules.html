Rules

  1. No botting or macroing.
  2. No using third party game clients.
  3. No abusing bugs.
  4. No playing multiple accounts at the same time.
  5. No sharing accounts.
  6. No impersonating RetroMMO staff.
  7. No harrassing other players.
  8. No hate speech.
  9. No spamming in chat.
  10. No real world trading.
  11. No using alternate accounts to evade rule enforcement.
  12. You may not register an account for RetroMMO or play RetroMMO if you are under the age of 13.

Moderators handle rule enforcement on a case by case basis. Your account may be modified, suspended or terminated at the discretion of RetroMMO staff at any time, regardless of if rules were broken.
