Rules

  1. No botting or macroing.
  2. No using third party game clients.
  3. No playing multiple accounts at the same time.
  4. No impersonating RetroMMO staff.
  5. No abusing bugs.
  6. No sharing accounts.
  7. No harrassing other players.

Moderators handle rule enforcement on a case by case basis. Your account may be modified, suspended or terminated at the discretion of RetroMMO staff at any time, regardless of if rules were broken.
